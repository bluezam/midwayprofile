My Origin Story

Hello, my name is Michael Bao. I was born in China but moved to the USA at the age of 6. So I'm culturally American, with a dash of eastern influence. I've been in the bay area my whole life. I've worked in several different industries. From being a cashier in services, sales in life insurance and currently IT. I am currently working at Pacific Engineering & Anaylst where we anaylst earthquakes to make interesting programs, buildings and bridges safer.

I'm currently diving deeper into coding and eventually hope to get into the web development or software industry. I'm studying to become a full stack developer at a bootcamp in UC Berkeley Extension. After that I hope to do more self studies and get into more data management. And maybe dive into dev ops.

On my free time I enjoy competitive sports and gaming. Following heavily in NBA and MMA. As for gaming I enjoy pretty much all genres from carefree RPGs and adventures. To highly competitive, intense and stressful RTS, MOBA, FPS, and Survival. I also casually lift weights, to lose weight and burn more calories while I'm gaming or working. What more can I ask for? I also like to cook and make delicious treats like a millie crepe cake.
